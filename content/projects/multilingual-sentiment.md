Fine-tuned audio classification model for translating dog barks into human-readable intent categories.

## Highlights

- Classifies barks into "hungry", "play", and "squirrel alert" with 89% accuracy.
- Trained on a curated dataset of 10,000 bark samples from 50 dog breeds.
- Released model weights and evaluation scripts on Hugging Face Hub.
